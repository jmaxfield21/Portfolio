A little bit about me

Me and my beautiful wife

I'm Jace. I've played soccer all my life (yes the background image is me) and more recently graduated college. I graduated with a degree in Computer Science and work professionally as a software engineer.

Most of my experience is in backend development and engineering. Since becoming a professional software engineer, I have developed a passion for clean, readable, maintainable code. I am currently expanding my skillset and trying to become a stronger front end web developer as well.

This page, and the links included above, are some of my front end work. A few of the pages are some of the first I tackled and are a little rough. But, some of my later work is far more polished and has given me the practice to be more effective on the front end of an application stack.
